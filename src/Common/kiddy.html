Board

Board games are one of the best, fun ways to support their development. The best board games for preschoolers are cooperative games that encourage them to take turns while developing other skills.

Cards

Playing card games is beneficial for dexterity and eye-hand coordination. The simple action of holding playing cards in their small hands helps children develop these two main motor skills.

Story

A storytelling game is a game where multiple players collaborate on telling a spontaneous story. Usually, each player takes care of one or more characters in the developing story.

Dance

Dance games are a subset of the larger genre of the Rhythm Game, which also includes popular music games such as Guitar Hero.Dance games can also be considered a part of the exergaming genre.
